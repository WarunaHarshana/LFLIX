### For Best TV Experience:
1. **Use DLNA** if your TV supports it
   - Start DLNA server on PC
   - TV auto-discovers "LocalFlix"
   - No browser needed!

2. **Use a streaming device**:
   - Chromecast
   - Fire TV Stick
   - Roku
   - Apple TV

3. **Connect laptop to TV**:
   - HDMI cable
   - Open LocalFlix on laptop
   - Full desktop experience on big screen!
